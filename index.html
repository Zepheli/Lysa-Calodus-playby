Lysa Calodus

A girl originally hailing from Doria, was a lovely place. Olive trees, grape vines, and picturesque villas. Surrounding larger cities of forums and culture. The people sworn to the Queendom of Aen, being one of its subjects in the long history of its never ending growth. It's time to see if this girl will dedicate to Her Majesty's cause. She has nothing else better to do.
